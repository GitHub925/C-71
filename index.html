Important Events

  • Weather Balloon Launch!

    Every year, HPMS has it's middle schoolers participate in an ultra-fun weather balloon project, building a weather balloon that climbs into space! Middle schooles add mini-cameras and surveillance tools and try to recover data right after the balloon crash-lands! This project is a student favorite when it comes to STEM!
